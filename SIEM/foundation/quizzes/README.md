## How to use them
1. Take the section straight after reading the matching knowledge-base file. Don't peek at answers.
2. Mark every question; review every wrong one against the KB and update your `weak-areas.md` log.
3. If you score ≤ 70% on a section, redo it 24h later before moving on.
4. Re-do the **whole quiz file** at the end of the relevant week as a quick recap.

For exam-style timed practice, see `foundation/simulations/`.
