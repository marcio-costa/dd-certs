# Daily 2-hour checklist (printable)

Use this template every study day. Tick boxes as you go.

---

## Date: ____________  Day #: ____  Domain focus: ____________

### Pre-flight (2 min)
- [ ] Open `foundation/knowledge-base/` to today's section
- [ ] Open `foundation/quizzes/` to today's quiz
- [ ] Phone in another room

### Block 1 — Read & note (50 min)
- [ ] Read the assigned KB section *carefully*
- [ ] Write a 1-page summary in your own words
- [ ] List 5 vocabulary terms introduced today

### Block 2 — Hands-on or video (50 min)
- [ ] If Datadog trial available: do the matching lab
- [ ] If not: watch the matching Datadog Learning Center video and replicate every example
- [ ] Take screenshots of key UI pages (you'll re-read them later)

### Daily mini-quiz (10 min)
- [ ] Take the 10-question Daily Mini-Quiz
- [ ] Review every wrong answer against the KB. Score: ____ / 10

### Reflection (10 min)
- [ ] Write 3 things you now understand that you didn't this morning
- [ ] Write 1 thing you still don't fully understand
- [ ] Add the gap to your `weak-areas.md` log

### End-of-day status
- [ ] All 4 blocks done
- [ ] Tomorrow's reading queued
- [ ] Sleep early, brain consolidates while you sleep
