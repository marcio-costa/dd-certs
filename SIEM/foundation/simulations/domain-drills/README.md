## Scoring rubric (out of 10 / 15 / 20)
- **≤ 60%** → Reread the matching knowledge-base file. Redo this drill in 2 days.
- **60–80%** → Note your wrong areas; redo only the missed concepts.
- **≥ 80%** → Domain is solid. Move on.

When a drill has been passed at ≥ 90% twice, you can safely skip it for the remainder of the program.
