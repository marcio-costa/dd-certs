# Daily Mini-Quiz Bank — 70 questions across all domains

This is the source bank for the **Daily Mini-Quiz (10 Qs)** simulation format.

**How to use:**
- Each study day, pick **10 questions at random** from this bank that cover the day's domain focus + a few review items.
- Track which questions you've seen in your `weak-areas.md` log so the rotation stays fresh.
- A simple selection rule:
  - 4 questions from today's primary domain
  - 4 questions from yesterday's domain (review)
  - 2 questions from a random earlier domain (long-term retention)

Questions are tagged with the domain ID. Answers are at the bottom.
