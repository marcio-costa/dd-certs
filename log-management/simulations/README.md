# Simulations — Overview

Four simulation formats are provided. Use them in this order during your study plan.

| Format | File(s) | Length | When to use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily mini-quiz** | `daily-mini-quizzes/day-01.md` … `day-07.md` | 10 Qs · ~12 min | Every day after reading. Mixed-domain — keeps recall warm. |
| **Domain-specific drill** | `domain-drills/drill-01-*.md` … `drill-07-*.md` | 15 Qs · ~25 min | After studying a domain end-to-end (or to retarget a weak domain). |
| **Adaptive weak-area drill** | `adaptive-weak-area-drill.md` | Variable (~25 Qs · 30 min) | Whenever your weak-areas log has 5+ items. Builds a custom drill from the question pool. |
| **Full mock exam** | `full-mock-exams/mock-exam-1.md`, `mock-exam-2.md` | 75 Qs · 2 hours | Once at the mid-point (Day 14) and once near the end (Day 25). Exam-realistic conditions. |

## Exam-realistic conditions for Mock Exams

- **Time-box.** Set a 2-hour timer. Don't pause.
- **No notes, no Datadog UI.** Closed book.
- **Mark, don't dwell.** Note any question you flag as uncertain, but don't go back during the timed run — finish first, review after.
- **Grade the next morning.** Sleep on it; you'll learn more from a calm review.
